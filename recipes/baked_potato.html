Baked Potato

Description

It's a potato. That has been baked. That's it.
This recipe provides 8 portions.

Ingredients

  • 8x Potato
  • 1x Coal (used as fuel)

Steps

  1. Harvest the potatos, multiple harvest may be required to obtain 8
  2. Put the potatos into the top slot of a furnace
  3. Add the coal into the bottom slot of a furnace
  4. Wait approximately 80 seconds
  5. Take the potato's out of the furnace
